Projects

Ultimate Captin Mich 

Optimization  

Research oriented, solving a modified version of the travelling salesman problem with additional constraints mixed with other optimization problems, like vertex cover and bin packing with a story-like problem description.

Tech: Python, Minizinc, matplotlib, numpy, Scipy(spatial.KDTree), Selenium, Tkinter, C++, threading, Flask.

Algorithms: Nearest neighbour, Nearest Insertion, Short cutting, Local search

  • Visualization of local search and all previously mentioned algorithms and much more!
  • Has two variants in Python & C++
  • A MineCraft demo (in-progress)
